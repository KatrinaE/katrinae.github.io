About Me

Fri 21 February 2014

I'm a current student at Hacker School in New York City. It's my first big commitment after a multi-year period of medical leave, during which programming was one of the (multiple) things I explored on my own. I applied to Hacker School because I felt like I would learn more if I was in a community of smart, helpful people.

I have a bunch of miscellaneous computer science/software experience. While I'm here, I want to:

  • Fill in some gaps (learn a statically typed language, use git correctly), finish a 'real' project,

  • Explore some cool stuff (right now I'm curious about algorithms, the Linux kernel, and distributed computing),

  • Generally sharpen my skills (I wrote in my application that I wanted to become a Python ninja).

I live with my husband in central Missouri. When I'm not at Hacker School, you'll find me traveling up and down the East Coast to visit all of my local friends and family.
